How to play:

1. Choose number of players from 2 to 6.
2. Enter namer of each player.
3. Enter your number, numbers cant repeat.
4. When your turn, throw a dice.

What is this all about:

Kosteczka is simple game similar to "bottle", you chose a number and when someone gets your number you have to do something.
